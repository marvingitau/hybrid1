About Hybrid AgriSupport

We've 5 years proven track record

In 2018, Hybrid Agricultural Support Limited was founded by our CEO, Philip, who harbored a deep-seated passion for agriculture and improving food security and the socioeconomic status of farmers. Our company was established in Naivasha with a clear goal in mind: to provide farmers with growth and expansion opportunities, easy access to farm inputs, and linkage to international markets.
